Programmability

Most mechanical keyboards will have software available to remap keys, make macros, etc. Software such as QMK/VIA have been developed to do these things, and can be accessed through the internet. Most keyboards are compatible with QMK/VIA, but some use their own built-in software to do these things, although sometimes in a slightly limited capacity.

This means that you can do things like make some cool shortcuts with keys or change the mapping of your keys to fit whatever is most comfortable to you!
